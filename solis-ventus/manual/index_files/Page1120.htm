Tips and Tricks:  Only “2X-Speed” effect is available, but you can easily ceate a “Half-Speed” effect:

      1.  Activate the 2X Speed effect.

      2.  Start recording.

      3.  Deactivate the 2X Speed effect - the loop you’ve just recorded will now play at half the speed.

 

  Recording a loop with 2X-Speed effect enabled halves the loop length limit to 20 seconds.
